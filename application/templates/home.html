Heart Attack & Disease Prediction
This Web Application predicts whether a person is likely to contract Heart Disease or succumb to a Heart Attack, or stay healthy.
This is done through the use of sklearn in python, which is used to train a classification prediction model.

The model takes in a total of 9 parameters:
HighBP, HighChol, BMI, Smoker, Stroke, Diabetes, HvyAlcoholConsump, Sex and Age
Predict Now

How is the model trained ?
The model is trained on a dataset consisting of 9 input variables and
one binary output variable: HeartDiseaseorAttack.
The original dataset consists of 22 columns and 250,000+ records of data.

However, only 10 of the variables were chosen for this task.
The model was trained using the 9 variables, and the feature importances
were plotted to show which variables affected the model the most.
